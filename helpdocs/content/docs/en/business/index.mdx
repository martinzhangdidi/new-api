## 💡 Our Original Intention

The original intention behind New API's creation is simple: **to make AI accessible to more people and lower the barrier to entry for AI**.

We believe that AI should not be a privilege for a few, but a tool accessible to everyone. Whether you are an independent developer, a startup team, or a large enterprise, you should be able to reduce internal access and management complexity under the premise of legal authorization and compliant deployment.

## 🤝 About Commercialization

Our choice to commercialize is not to maximize profit, but to enable the project to **develop healthily and sustainably**.

Servers incur costs, maintenance requires effort, and ecosystem development needs resources—moderate commercialization is a necessary guarantee for New API to continue serving the community. We pledge to invest profits back into product iteration, community building, and ecosystem expansion, ensuring that every bit of support translates into better services.

## 📜 Gentleman's Agreement

The New API community has always upheld the concept of a **Gentleman's Agreement**:

- We trust every user and provide flexible and open ways of use.
- We do not impose cumbersome restrictions, trusting that everyone will use resources responsibly.
- We expect users, while benefiting, to also respect the sustainable development of the project.
- If your project benefits from New API, we welcome contributions back to the community in any form.

This is an agreement based on trust and respect, and a continuation of the open-source spirit.
