Here you can perform internal balance management, authorization redemption, and compliance invitation records. Payment methods are configured by the administrator in System Settings -> Payment Settings.

<Callout type="warn">
  Top-ups, redemption codes, and invitation links are applicable to legally authorized scenarios. When used, they should comply with the authorization scope, upstream service terms, platform rules, and local laws and regulations.
</Callout>
